The Elements of Style

Challenge

To conceptualize and design the layout of The Elements of Style by E.B. White and William Strunk Jr.

Solution

In the foreword, E.B. White talks about Strunk's desire to write all the rules of proper writing and condense them onto the head of a pin. I used this idea as the basis for my concept and layout. I created the hole punch pages by hand using a Japanese hold punch. These pages show how tiny pin-pricks come together to create the title of the section or chapter. The elongated shape of the book alludes to the tall, narrow shape of a pin.
