Database Visualization Poster

Challenge

This is part one of a two part project. The first part was to collect a database of one hundred things and to make a poster that showed the one hundred items organized by attributes unique to the collection.

Solution

I chose one hunred ceramics pieces I had created as my collection. I made icons to represent each ceramic piece, which were narrowed down to bowls, vases and mugs. I thought about my process when creating these pieces and decided to organize them into a family tree.
