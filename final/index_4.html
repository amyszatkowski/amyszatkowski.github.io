How to Save the Bees Calendar

Challenge

To create create a three page calendar with a cover that gives information about "how to" respond to a social issue.

Solution

I decided to make my calendar about saving the honeybee. Honeybees are dying off at a rapid pace and I wanted to bring this issue to people's attention and let them know how they can help keep the busy bees busy. I used the honeycomb pattern to bring in eye-catching images that relate to the bee's situation. There are three steps, one per page, that give information about how to help save the bees.
