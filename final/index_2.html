Ten Things About Antioxidants

Challenge

To create an informative booklet giving ten pieces of information about a topic relating to nurition.

Solution

I decided to focus on antioxidants. Whenever I go shopping see packaging promoting antioxidants, from granola bars to hair dye, and I wanted to know what all the hype was about. I found that natural foods are much more beneficial than supplements and processed foods. But people still buy the antioxidant-labeled products even though they don't have all the information. This led to me use collage imagery. The pieced together images made me think of how advertisements only give bits and pieces of information instead of the whole picture.
