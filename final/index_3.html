Enchroma Rebranding

Challenge

To create create a logo for EnChroma colorblind correcting sunglasses.

Solution

EnChroma sunglasses use a special coating and layering technology to create lenses that allow people who are colorblind to see colors correctly. The EnChroma website has testimonials from people who have tried the glasses and their reactions are full of wonder and amazement. I wanted the logo to have that same sense of wide-eyed wonder while also remaining connected to the science behind the glasses. The form of the logo looks like an eye seen from the side, wide open. The colors are being directed towards the eye. The shape also forms an "E" for EnChroma.
